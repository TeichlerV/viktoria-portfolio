Overview

Designed and developed a professional website for a Swedish painter, improving the business's credibility and online presence.

Research

Thoroughly analyzed the regional competition, consisting mainly of larger companies with practical-oriented websites. I, therefore, wanted this website to stand out, not only with a modern design but also with its simplicity and visual appeal to clients.

Design

Created an elegant and visually appealing website highlighting the painter's expertise in craftsmanship. The main image is therefore composed to show in an elegant way that the painter is good at both hand painting, pattern matching wallpapering, and working with color. Simplicity and visual appeal were prioritized to attract potential clients.

User Experience

Tested the website with users, ensuring easy navigation and an intuitive layout. Selected engaging colors and fonts for an enjoyable browsing experience.

Results

The painter loved the final design. The website achieved the goal of making the business look more serious and professional. People who visited the website found it easy, aesthetically appealing and gave positive responses.

Medium

Overview

Successfully designed and developed a website for a medium in Sweden. The client's priority was to create a user-friendly platform that could provide detailed information about her new business.

Research

An analysis was conducted to see other mediums and psychics' websites and how they engage with their visitors. Most websites were white and my client wanted a more powerful website with her favorite color to stand out.

Design

The client describe herself as an ordinary mother of three and it was important to her to have a simple website. The logo incorporated her favorite flower, adding a personal touch, alongside a spiritual image to showcase the essence of her business. We aimed to create a balance between a mystical atmosphere and avoiding anything too creepy. This was accomplished by using a purple background, tasteful black details, and pictures.

User Experience

While the website has yet to be made public, we shared it with the client's friends and family for feedback. The response was overwhelmingly positive, with everyone appreciating how the site authentically showcased the client's personality.

Results

The goal to make the website cozy and personalized was achieved and the client was very satisfied with the results. She is looking forward to sharing it with the world and I am to hear about any feedback she will receive.
